### Slide 2: Introduction

*(Play Wizard Chess video clip)*

That was the wizard chess from Harry Potter — pieces move on their own and smash each other when capturing. We want to build something similar. We can't do the smashing part, but at least the pieces will move by themselves.

---

### Slide 3: Purpose of the System

The basic idea is: there's an XY platform under the board with an electromagnet. Each piece has an iron plate at the bottom. When powered, the electromagnet grabs and drags the piece.

A camera above captures the board, and the system recognizes the position and controls piece movement.

Our goal is a demo system that performs basic piece movements.
